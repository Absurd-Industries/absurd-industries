The Manifesto

Why we exist. What we believe. No fine print.

Open it? Warranty intact.

Every product ships with full schematics, BOM, firmware source, and CAD files. Your purchase, your right.

Break it while learning? We're proud.

Community support, repair guides, and spare parts. We designed it to be understood, not just used.

Improve our design? Please do.

Fork it, mod it, make it better. Contributions go back to the community. That's the whole point.

Access to the actual maker? Obviously.

No faceless corporation. Talk directly to the person who designed and built your hardware.
