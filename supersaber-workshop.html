Welcome, glorious nerds

This is a two-day hands-on workshop combining the genius of lightsabers with the brilliance of open-source hardware!

What you’ll learn

Building a Supersaber levels up a bunch of skills!

  1. Electronics: a crash course in working with a best-in-class microcontroller and industrial-grade Li-ion battery.
  2. Programming: rapid and flexible programming with CircuitPython.
  3. Assembly: hands-on experience putting together a complex, multi-part project.
  4. Light: an excellent way to play with light!
  5. Wielding: channel the force and learn how to wield your Supersaber!
