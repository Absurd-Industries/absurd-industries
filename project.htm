Lampy: Cyberpunk Workspace Light

An introduction to addressable LEDs in Arduino by making a cyberpunk workspace lamp.

Updated on 17:57, Sat, 27th Jan, 2023

Arduino, WS2812B, ESP32C3, XIAO, Lighting, Neopixels, BLE, 3D Printing

Features

  • Interactive Light Modes: Lampy boasts various light modes, each designed to fit different moods and settings
  • Smooth Transitions: Utilizing easing algorithms, Lampy ensures that the transition between light modes is smooth and visually seamless
  • High-Density LED: Powered by 72 Neopixel LEDs capable of over 16 million colors

Bill of Materials

Here are the materials you need to make a Lampy!

1 XIAO ESP32C3 + Antenna
½ meter WS2812B Addressable RGB 10mm LED Strip
