WELCOME, KEEPER OF LIGHT

What You'll Learn

Creating a Bloom levels up a bunch of skills!

  1. Electronics: Get a crash course in working with a best-in-class microcontrollery
  2. Programming: Learn rapid and flexible programming with Arduino
  3. Assembly: Hands-on experience in putting together a complex, multi-part lamp
  4. Light: An excellent way for playing with light!
  5. Patterns: Create infinite patterns that light up your space in unique ways!

What you'll be working with

XIAO ESP32C3

Work with one of the best RISC-V microcontollers out there, with WiFi and BLE

Neopixels

Highest density addressable WS2812B LEDs for butter smooth transitions

Arduino

Arduino is a fantastic tool and environment for beginners, hobbyists and veterans

Patterns

Master the art of lighting your space with Bloom patterns
