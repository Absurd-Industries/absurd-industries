Understand the essential components of drones and drone flight, then nurture your hatchling from box to race-ready flyer.

Drone Flight 101

You’ll be learning VLOS (visual line-of-sight) flying:

  1. Basics of safe take-off and smooth landing.
  2. Strafing, changing heading, and controlling speed.
  3. Master the mid-air flip and impress your homies.
  4. Ins and outs of drone care and safety.
