Burning Questions

Things you're probably wondering about this weird approach to learning

Is this real education or just messing around?

Both! "Just messing around" is how most breakthroughs happen. The world's best scientists, engineers, and artists all describe their work as "play." We've just structured that play to ensure you actually learn something.

The most successful people in any field are the ones who never stopped treating it like a fascinating game. We're helping you build that mindset from the start.

How do you measure success without tests?

Did you make something that works? Can you explain how and why it works? Can you teach someone else to do it? That's our measure of success.

Traditional tests measure your ability to memorize information temporarily. We'd rather measure your ability to create something real that demonstrates your understanding. That's what matters in the real world anyway.

Is the Knowledge Coin thing for real?

Absolutely! We never want money to be a barrier to learning. If you can't afford a course, you can take our 3D-printed Knowledge Coin instead. The "cost" is your commitment to teach what you've learned to at least one other person and pass the coin to them.

Each coin has a unique tracking number. We're building a system to map how far each coin travels and how many people learn through this chain reaction of knowledge sharing.
