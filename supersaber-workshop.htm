What You'll Learn

Building a Supersaber levels up a bunch of skills!

  1. Electronics: Get a crash course in working with a best-in-class microcontroller and industrial-grade Li-ion battery
  2. Programming: Learn rapid and flexible programming with CircuitPython
  3. Assembly: Hands-on experience in putting together a complex, multi-part project
  4. Light: An excellent way for playing with light!
  5. Wielding: Channel the force and learn how to wield your Supersaber!
