What You'll Learn

Building a Supersaber hones a bunch of skills!

  1. Electronics: Get a crash course in working with the best-in-class microcontroller current available. Impress your friends!
  2. Programming: CircuitPython is a highly readable and flexible programming language that's ideal for building out wild ideas
  3. Assembly: Hands-on experience in putting together a complex, awe-inspiring project. It’s like playing God, but more hands-on!
  4. Light: An astounding good build for for working/experimenting with light
  5. Wielding: Courtesy of Supervillains, you’ll learn to wield your SuperSaber
