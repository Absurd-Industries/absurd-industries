Policies

Refund Policy

At Absurd Industries, we believe in the quality of our absurdly innovative products and the satisfaction of our customers. However, if our products don't light up your life the way you expected, here’s our policy on refunds:

For Tangible Products:

  1. Items must be returned within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.
  2. Products must be returned in their original condition, with all accessories included.
  3. Shipping costs are non-refundable, except in cases where we've made a shipping error.

For Workshops and Events:

  1. Cancellations made 7 days prior to the event will receive a 100% refund.
  2. Cancellations made 3-6 days prior will incur a 50% cancellation fee.
  3. No refunds will be issued for cancellations made within 48 hours of the event start time.

Refund Process:

To initiate a refund, contact us at refunds@absurd.industries, with your order number and reason for return.

Refunds will be processed to the original method of payment within 7 days of receiving the returned item or cancellation notice.

FAQs:

  • Q: Can I get a refund if I change my mind?
    A: Yes, as long as you return the product within 30 days in its original condition.
  • Q: How long do refunds take?
    A: Refunds are processed within 14 days after we receive your returned item or cancellation notice.

Shipping Policy

We ship our innovative goods around the city, ensuring they reach you efficiently and securely.

Domestic Shipping:

  1. Standard shipping takes 1-2 business days.
  2. Expedited shipping options are available at an additional cost.

International Shipping:

We currently do not ship internationally.

FAQs:

  • Q: Do you provide tracking information?
    A: Yes, once your order is shipped, we’ll send you tracking details.
  • Q: What happens if my order is lost in transit?
    A: Fret not, contact us and we'll investigate. If we confirm it's lost, we’ll replace it or refund you.

Return Policy

If you're not over the moon with your purchase, here's how you can send it back to us:

  1. Returns must be made within 30 days of purchase.
  2. Items must be in new and unused condition, with all original packaging.
  3. Customers are responsible for return shipping costs, unless the item is defective or incorrect.

FAQs:

  • Q: How do I return a product?
    A: Contact us for a return authorization. Then, ship the product back to us with a traceable method.
  • Q: What if the item is damaged when I receive it?
    A: Report the issue within 48 hours with photos, and we’ll sort it out faster than a light particle zips through space.
