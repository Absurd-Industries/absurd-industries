Refund Policy

At Absurd Industries, we pride ourselves on innovation and fun, but hey, we get it – sometimes things don't work out. Here's the lowdown on refunds:

For Tangible Products:

  • Change of heart? No problem. Just return items within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.
  • All we ask is that the products are in their original condition, unloved and unused, like a brand new gadget.
  • If we goofed up the shipping, we'll bear those costs because that's on us.

For Workshops and Events:

  • Can't make it? Cancel up to 7 days in advance for a full refund - no side-eye, we promise.
  • Less than a week's notice? You'll get back 50% - let's call it a "change-of-heart" fee.
  • Within 48 hours? That’s cutting it close! No refunds, but we’re all ears if you have a time travel solution.

How to Request a Refund:

Need a refund? Just shoot us an email at refunds@absurd.industries with your order number and a bit about why. We'll get your refund rolling within 7 days of getting your message.

FAQs:

  • Q: Can I return a product if I change my mind?

  • Q: How fast do you process refunds?
